I am a Bioinformatician with experience in microbial genomics, phylogenetics, genomic epidemiology, and web-based visualisation tools development. My research focuses on understanding the evolutionary and genomic epidemiology of multi-drug resistant bacteria in healthcare settings. In the last six years, I have constantly been working on processing and analysing bacterial genome datasets using various bioinformatics tools and pipelines. I am proficient in programming and web-based visualisation tools development using Unix commands, R, Python and JavaScript. My passion is to produce and perform translatable genomic research to improve healthcare products and services.
