Hello, my name is Budi. I am a bioinformatician and data visualization enthusiast. I have a Bachelor of Science Degree in Biology from the Bandung Institute of Technology Indonesia. In 2016, I continued my study in Master of Bioinformatics program at University of Queensland. 

During my master study, I worked on a research project for developing [HAIviz](https://haiviz.beatsonlab.com), an interactive web application for the visualization of Healthcare-Associated Infections (HAIs) outbreak analysis, supervised by [A/Prof Scott Beatson](https://scmb.uq.edu.au/profile/312/scott-beatson) and [Dr. Brian Forde](https://clinical-research.centre.uq.edu.au/profile/5433/brian-forde)

From there on, I joined the [Beatson Lab](https://clinical-research.centre.uq.edu.au/profile/5433/brian-forde) and started my PhD in 2018 to extend his research in exploring the computation model for genomic and epidemiological of HAIs in Queensland hospitals. My projects focus on developing genomic analysis and visualization tools for investigating multidrug resistant bacterial outbreaks in hospital settings.
